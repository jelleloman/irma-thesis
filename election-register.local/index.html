IRMA election registration demo

Welcome to the registration page for this IRMA election. Here, you can request a voting card to use in the upcoming election. This requires two steps:

  1. Confirming your identity
  2. Retrieving your voting card
Both are done by scanning a QR code.

Let's start with confirming your identity. To do this, you need to have the IRMA app installed on your phone. Once you have that, click to button below to get started.
