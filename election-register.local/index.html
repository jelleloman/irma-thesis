IRMA election registration demo

Welcome to the registration page for this election. Here, you can request a voting card to use in the upcoming election. This requires two steps that are done by scanning a QR code:

  1. Confirming your identity
  2. Retrieving your voting card

Confirming your identity

Let's start with confirming your identity. To do this, you need to have the IRMA app installed on your phone. Once you have that, click the button below to get started.

Because this is a demo website, there is no backend that keeps track of eligible voters along with their attributes. Your identity is 'confirmed' by evaluating your pbdf.pbdf.email.email attribute. If you want to retrieve a voting card, enter an email address you have loaded in your IRMA app here.
