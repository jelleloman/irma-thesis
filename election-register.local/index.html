IRMA election registration demo

This demo showcases the potential flow for registering for a digital election with the IRMA framework. Once an election has been set up, users can register to vote on this page by showing ownership of the attributes needed to participate in the election.

For this example, only the possession of a pbdf.pbdf.email.email attribute is checked. Enter your email address below, and click on 'Register' to receive a mock-up voting card.
