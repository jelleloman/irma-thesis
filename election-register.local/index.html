IRMA election registration demo

Welcome to the registration page for this election. Here, you can request a voting card to use in the upcoming election. This requires two steps:

  1. Confirming your identity
  2. Retrieving your voting card
Both are done by scanning a QR code.

Confirming your identity

Let's start with confirming your identity. To do this, you need to have the IRMA app installed on your phone. Once you have that, click the button below to get started.
