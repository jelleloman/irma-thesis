Cast your vote

This demo site shows the vote casting process for digital elections with IRMA. If you already have a mock-up voting card after registering here, you can select an option below and click 'Vote'. This will prompt you to confirm your choice.

If you confirm, you are prompted to scan a QR code to sign your choice. Your vote will be shown in the mobile application in natural language before you confirm your signature.
